Patrick Shriwise

Graduate student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison.

BIO

I grew up in Kansas City and graduated from the Mechanical Engineerig Dept. of Kansas State University in Manhattan, KS. I interned at several engineering firms only to find that research was my passion. More specifically, research in the nuclear sciences. I spent my final undergraduate year doing research with Dr. John Schlup as part of an interdisciplinary project in oncology. I then moved to Madison to do research on plasma equilibria, magnetic topology design and coil fabrication with the Pegasus Group at UW. After receiving my masters degree, I then moved into the CNERG group in the pursuit of my PhD under Paul Wilson. My experience in this group has been very rewarding. I've had the opportunity to collaborate with scientists at Argonne National Laboratory in developtment of the SIGMA Project and Matthew Turk at the University of Illinois in development of the yt visualization toolkit .
